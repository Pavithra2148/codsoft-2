About Us

Welcome to Ponni's Hub, an extraordinary culinary haven situated in the heart of the city. Since our inception, Ponni's Hub has been a cherished destination for aficionados seeking the pinnacle of gastronomic delight. At Ponni's Hub, we aspire to be more than just a restaurant; we aim to be a conduit for cultural celebration and culinary exploration. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the kitchen; we strive to foster a warm and welcoming ambiance where every guest feels embraced by the spirit of South Indian hospitality. Through community engagement initiatives and partnerships with local artisans and farmers, we endeavor to enrich and uplift our community. Ponni's Hub is honored to have received accolades such as "Best South Indian Restaurant" by Foodie Magazine and recognition from esteemed culinary authorities. Join us on a journey of flavors as we invite you to savor the essence of South India at Ponni's Hub.
